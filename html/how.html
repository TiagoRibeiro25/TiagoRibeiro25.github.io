    • How to Use

    This is a simple web application that allows you to manage your anime collection.

    To add an anime to your collection, simply click on the "Add New" button and fill in the required fields.

    To remove an anime from your collection, simply click on the "Delete" button and choose the anime that will be deleted.

    To edit an anime in your collection, simply click on the "Open" button of the desired anime and change the information.

    To change your password, simply click on the "Account Settings" button and then "Settings" and type your new password twice.
